Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 07, 2026

At TaskBar, operated by VKNOVA TECHGROUPS (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED, we are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a secure reward environment.

1. Introduction

This policy complies with the Google Play Developer Policy (2026) and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules. It explains how we handle your data with integrity and transparency.

2. Data Collection & Purpose

To maintain the integrity of our reward system and prevent "Invalid Traffic," we collect:

  • Account Information: Email Address and Username (via Google/Firebase Auth) for account creation and cloud synchronization of your "Bolts" balance.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect the Android ID and Advertising ID.
  • Purpose: Fraud prevention, enforcing the "One Account Per Device" rule, and preventing auto-clicker/bot abuse.
  • App Activity: We track "Bolt" earnings and ad interactions to verify task completion and process payouts.
3. Anti-Fraud & Security (Your Protection)

TaskBar uses active monitoring to ensure a fair ecosystem. We detect and block:

  • VPNs & Proxies to prevent geo-spoofing.
  • Auto-Clickers/Macros to prevent bot-driven "Bolt" farming.
  • Rooted/Emulated Devices to prevent system-level hacking.

Note: Use of these tools results in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of all rewards.

4. Mandatory Account Deletion

In accordance with Google Play’s Data Deletion Policy, users have the right to request the permanent removal of their data.

  • In-App Deletion: Use the "Delete Account" button in the Settings menu.
  • Web-Based Deletion: Click here to request deletion. or Visit https://novatechgroups.github.io/nova-tech-groups/delete-account.html

Outcome: All associated data (Email, Android ID, Bolt Balance) will be purged from Firebase and our servers within 30 days.
